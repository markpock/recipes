Aglio e Oglio

Description

A simple, lovely pasta dish which brings out the best in its few ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Pasta of any shape, squared spaghettoni preferred.
  • A good extra virgin olive oil.
  • About half a head of garlic.
  • Red pepper flakes, Calabrian if possible.
  • Parmigiano reggiano - if not possible, other cheeses work.
  • Italian parsley.
  • Black pepper.

Steps

  1. Bring water to a rolling boil in a shallow pot or wide saucepan, salting liberally.
  2. Slice garlic thinly as possible.
  3. Immerse pasta in boiling water.
  4. Fry shaved garlic in extra virgin olive oil over medium-low heat until garlic takes on light coloration.
  5. Crack pepper over the oil, toasting it gently.
  6. When pasta is several minutes before al dente, remove with tongs to pan with garlic, reserving the residual starchy water.
  7. Toss pasta in garlic-flavoured oil, using starchy water to create an emulsion.
  8. When complete, grate parmigiano reggiano and pluck parsley leaves over the dish.
  9. Serve.
