Bistecca alla Fiorentina

Description

The hearty preparation of steak native to Tuscany and known the world over.

Ingredients

  • A thick-cut porterhouse steak from a steer or heifer dry-aged for two weeks or more.

Steps

  1. Bring the steak, yet unseasoned, to room temperature.
  2. Sear the steak hard for three or so minutes close to a charcoal grill.
  3. Flip using tongs, gripping lightly, and sear the other side.
  4. Bring the temperature down (or the steak further away from the coals) and stand the steak up on its flat bone.
  5. Let stand for five to seven minutes.
  6. Serve.
