Tiramisu

Description

A rich, creamy dessert mingling the dark, fruity flavors of coffee and the luscious sweetness of mascarpone.

Ingredients

  • Egg yolks.
  • Cane sugar (medium-brown preferred, e.g. demerara or turbinado).
  • Mascarpone cheese.
  • Ristretto cooled down.
  • Dark rum or marsala wine.
  • Several ladyfingers.
  • A chocolate bar or cocoa powder.

Steps

  1. Beat egg yolks and sugar together.
  2. Add mascarpone and ristretto, mixing until combined.
  3. Mix more ristretto with rum or marsala in a separate shallow dish.
  4. Dip each ladyfinger into the mixture, reserving one layer in a baking dish.
  5. Spread the mascarpone mixture over this first layer.
  6. Create a second layer using the same procedure.
  7. Top up with remaining mascarpone mixture.
  8. Cover with seran wrap and refrigerate.
  9. Garnish with shaved chocolate or dust with cocoa powder.
  10. Serve.
